Solar Toy Car

This Project is a solar powered car that moves using only energy from the sun. It was designed to show how renewable energy can produce motion without using batteries or fuel.

A solar car is a small vehicle that runs directly on solar energy. The sunlight is captured by a solar panel, converted into electricity, and used to power a motor that makes the car move.

üöó How it works

When sunlight reaches the solar panel, it produces electric energy. That energy flows through the wires to the electric motor, which turns the gears connected to the wheels, making the car move forward. The car only works when it receives enough direct sunlight.

Wires

Electrical conductors that connect the solar panel to the motor. They carry electric current. If the wires are reversed, the motor turns in the opposite direction.

Gears

Small toothed wheels that transmit movement from the motor to the wheels. The small gear from the motor turns the big gear, increasing power and making the wheels spin.

Electric motor

A device that changes electrical energy into movement. When it gets electricity from the solar panel, it spins and moves the gears.

Solar panel

A plate that converts sunlight into electricity. When it receives sunlight, it produces electric current that powers the motor.
